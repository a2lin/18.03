######1a)
$$
\begin{align}
\ddot{x} + \omega^2x &= 0 \\\
\bbox[5px, border:2px solid black]{r^2 + \omega^2} &= 0\\\
r^2 &= -\omega^2\\\
r &= \bbox[5px, border:2px solid black]{\pm i\omega}
\end{align}
$$
Exponential solutions:
$$
 \bbox[5px, border:2px solid black]{C_1cos(\omega t) + C_2sin(\omega t)}
$$
