######2a)
$$
\begin{align}
{d\over dt} + 2x(t) &= 1 \\\
{d\over dt} &= 1 - 2x(t)\\\
\int {1\over 1-2x}\ dx &= \int 1\ dt \\\
-{1\over 2} ln(1-2x(t)) &= t + C_0\\\
ln(1-2x(t)) &= -2t + C_0\\\
1 - 2x(t) &= Ce^{-2t} \\\
-2x(t) &= Ce^{-2t}-1 \\\
x(t) &= \bbox[5px, border:2px solid black]{Ce^{-2t}+{1\over2}}\\\
\end{align}
